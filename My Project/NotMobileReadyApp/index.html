This is a test to check the Google Mobile Friendliness of your website.

The Google Mobile-friendly Tester is a vital tool for you to use to check the mobile-friendliness of your websites and provides additional information sourced from all official Google data. This tool even provides more information than Google’s own tool.

If your website is not mobile-friendly, this checker will tell you. If your website checker response is “FAILED”, then you must get your website redesigned to be mobile-friendly or you will likely lose ALL of your mobile traffic.

This Google Mobile-friendly Tester includes a bulk testing feature in which you can upload a list of URLs and download the test results into a CSV file!
